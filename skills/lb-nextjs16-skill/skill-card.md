## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Documentation examples may be copied into production without enough review for authentication, authorization, token handling, telemetry redaction, remote debugging exposure, third-party scripts, MCP access, or accessibility settings. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt examples against the application's security, privacy, and accessibility requirements before use. <br>
Risk: The skill is a static documentation reference and may not reflect newer Next.js behavior after the packaged release. <br>
Mitigation: Check the release version and compare critical guidance against current project requirements before making framework or deployment decisions. <br>
