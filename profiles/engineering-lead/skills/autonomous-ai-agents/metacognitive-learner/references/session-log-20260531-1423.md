## Gap Identified

Layer 2.5 (automated SOP validation) is missing. This is why "learn → improve" loops don't actually improve behavior — SOPs exist but aren't enforced.

**Root cause from CRITIC (ICLR 2024)**: LLM cannot reliably self-verify. Validation must be external-triggered.
